About the Founder

Meet Abdullah

"Thank you for choosing Westwood Landscapes."

My name is Abdullah Jamawe and I am a high school student with a genuine passion for making our community a cleaner, greener, more beautiful place to live.

I started Westwood Landscapes because I noticed neighbors with demanding schedules, elderly residents, and people recovering from injuries struggling to keep up with their gardens. I wanted to make a real difference — and make it affordable for everyone.

My mission is simple: improve the community one garden at a time. I am proud to be a neighbor-first landscaper who genuinely cares about the people I serve right here in Westwood.
