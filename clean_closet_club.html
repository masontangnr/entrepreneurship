Our Mission

Where Compassion Meets Action

We bridge the gap between those with too much and those in need — making giving simple, dignified, and meaningful for everyone.

At Clean Closet Club, we believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and cared for — especially during life's most challenging chapters. We collect gently used clothing and accessories from donors right at their doorstep and thoughtfully deliver them to individuals and families in need through our partnership with Clothed by Faith.

What sets us apart is the heart we put into every step. Recipients don't just receive a bag of clothes — they receive a beautifully wrapped purple gift bag, because dignity matters just as much as the donation itself.

How It Works

From Your Closet to Their Confidence

A thoughtful, seamless process — from your front door all the way to a beautifully wrapped gift in someone's hands.

1
🚗

Donation Pickup

We drive directly to your home and collect your gently used clothing and accessories. No hassle, no extra trips — we make giving effortless for you.

2
🏠

Transport to Charity

All collected items are carefully transported to our partner, Clothed by Faith, where the sorting process begins.

3
👗

Careful Sorting

Items are sorted with care — those ready to be gifted are organized by size and category, while others are sent to be recycled into new materials.

4
📋

A Request Is Received

We receive a request from someone in need, gathering details about their size, preferences, and needs so we can make their gift truly personal.

5
🛍️

Personal Shopping

We "go shopping" through our curated collection, hand-selecting items that fit and suit that specific person — making each gift feel truly chosen for them.

6
🎁

Wrapped & Sent with Love

Items are beautifully packaged in our signature purple gift bag and sent off — because everyone deserves to feel like they're receiving a real gift.

Meet the Founder

Lauren Carlsen

My name is Lauren Carlsen. I am a 16-year-old pageant queen, dedicated to serving my Texas community. I have a passion for this movement because I have seen friends and family in a time of their life where basic necessities like clothes were not available.

Clean Closet Club works to restore dignity and peace on the giving and receiving end. I started this journey three years ago with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to feel cared for, confident, and seen — no matter what season of life they're walking through.

Clothes that can't be repurposed are recycled into thread, rope, and even rubber padding on playgrounds — because nothing goes to waste when there's a purpose behind it.
