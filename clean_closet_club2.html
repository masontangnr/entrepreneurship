How It Works

From Your Closet to Someone's Fresh Start

A simple, thoughtful process that honors both the donor and the recipient every step of the way.

🚗 1
Pickup
We drive to your home and collect your gently used clothes and accessories.
🏠 2
Transport
Donations are carefully transported to Clothed by Faith.
👕 3
Sort & Assess
We sort all items — wearable pieces are set aside; others go to recycling.
📋 4
Receive Request
A request comes in from someone in need, with their sizes and preferences.
🛍️ 5
"Shopping" Trip
We personally curate a selection tailored to each recipient's unique needs.
🎁 6
Wrap with Love
Clothes are beautifully wrapped in our signature purple gift bag.
💜 7
Delivered
The gift is delivered, restoring confidence at the start of a new chapter.

Meet the Founder

Lauren Carlsen

"I have seen friends and family in a time of their life where basic necessities like clothes were not available. Clean Closet Club works to restore dignity and peace on the giving and receiving end."

My name is Lauren Carlsen. I am a 16-year-old pageant queen, dedicated to serving my Texas community. I have a passion for this movement because I have seen firsthand what it means to struggle with accessing basic necessities — and I believe every person deserves to feel seen, cared for, and valued.

Over the past three years, I have donated 3,000+ items to people in need — not just clothes, but confidence, stability, and a fresh start. Every purple gift bag is packed with care because the way we give matters just as much as what we give.

Clothes that can't be repurposed are recycled into thread, rope, and even rubber padding on playgrounds — because nothing should go to waste when it can still serve a purpose in our community.
